At Skailar, we strive to provide high-quality products and services. However, please be aware of our refund policies before making a purchase.

### No Refund Policy

All purchases made through Skailar are non-refundable, and all sales are considered final. This policy applies to all products and services, including but not limited to enhanced software and accounts. Once a purchase is completed, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

### Replacements

In the event of a severe technical issue that renders the product unusable, we offer a one-time replacement. This replacement includes:
- A new license key for the same product.
- Time compensation to ensure you receive the full value of your purchase.

To request a replacement:
- Contact our [support team](mailto:support@skailar.com) with details of the technical issue.
- Provide any necessary documentation or evidence to support your claim (as Order ID).
- Our team will review your request and, if approved, will arrange for the new license key and time compensation.

### Need Assistance?

If you have any questions about our refund policies or need further assistance, please contact our [support team](mailto:support@skailar.com). We're here to help!

Thank you for understanding and for choosing Skailar.
