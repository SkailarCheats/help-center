At Skailar, we offer a variety of payment options to ensure a smooth and convenient purchasing experience. You can choose from the following accepted payment methods:

### PayPal
PayPal is a secure and widely used payment method. To use PayPal:
- Select PayPal as your payment option during checkout.
- You will be redirected to the PayPal login page.
- Log in with your PayPal credentials and confirm the payment.

### Credit Card
We accept major credit cards for your convenience. To pay with a credit card:
- Select the credit card option during checkout.
- Enter your card details, including the card number, expiration date, and CVV.
- Review your order and click "Place Order" to complete the transaction.

### Cryptocurrency
We support payments with Bitcoin (BTC) and Litecoin (LTC). To pay with cryptocurrency:
- Select the cryptocurrency option during checkout.
- Choose either Bitcoin (BTC) or Litecoin (LTC).
- You will be provided with a wallet address and the exact amount to send.
- Use your crypto wallet to complete the payment by sending the specified amount to the given address.

### Need Assistance?
If you encounter any issues with your payment or have any questions, please contact our [support team](mailto:support@skailar.com). We're here to help!
