If you wish to close your Skailar account, please follow these steps to initiate the process:

### Closing Your Account Process

1. **Contact Skailar Support**
   - To close your account, you need to contact our support team directly.
   - Send an email to [support@skailar.com](mailto:support@skailar.com) or use our Discord server at [discord.gg/skailar](https://discord.gg/skailar) to request account closure.

2. **Verification**
   - Our support team will verify your identity to ensure account ownership and security.
   - Be prepared to provide information to assist in confirming your identity and account details.

3. **Account Closure**
   - Once your identity is verified, our support team will proceed with closing your Skailar account.

### Important Information

<Warning>
**Data Removal:** Closing your account may involve the deletion of personal data associated with your account, subject to our [privacy policy](https://skailar.com/legal/terms).
</Warning>
<Note>
**License and Subscription:** Closing your account does not automatically cancel any active licenses or subscriptions. Please manage these separately if needed.
</Note>
