Keep your Skailar account details up-to-date by following these steps to update your personal information through the settings panel on our website.

### Updating Your Information

1. **Log In to Your Account**
   - Visit [skailar.com](https://skailar.com) and log in to your account using your credentials.

2. **Access Account Settings**
   - Navigate to the settings panel or account dashboard, usually accessible from the top navigation menu or a dedicated settings icon.

3. **Edit Personal Details**
   - Locate the section where you can update your personal information, such as your name, email address, or password.

4. **Save Changes**
   - After making the necessary updates, ensure to save your changes by following the prompts or clicking on the "Save" button.

### Security Reminder

- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Consider enabling 2FA for added security. This extra layer of protection helps safeguard your account from unauthorized access.
