If you've forgotten your password for your Skailar account, please follow these steps to initiate the password recovery process:

### Password Recovery Process

1. **Contact Skailar Support**
   - To recover your password, you need to [contact](support/contacting-technical-support) our support team directly.
   - Send an email to [support@skailar.com](mailto:support@skailar.com) indicating that you need assistance with password recovery.

2. **Verification**
   - For security reasons, our support team will verify your identity to ensure account ownership.
   - Be prepared to provide information that helps us confirm your identity and ownership of the account.

3. **Password Reset**
   - Once your identity is verified, our support team will assist you in resetting your password securely.
